About the Firm

A Litigation Background Behind Every Transaction

I am the founder and president of Recalde Law Firm, P.A. My practice focuses on business transactions involving changes in ownership, capital structure, or control — including formations, financings, acquisitions, restructurings, and exits.

I represent founders, investors, and closely held businesses across a broad range of transactional matters. My background includes both transactional work and complex commercial litigation — a perspective that informs how I structure and document transactions, with attention to risk allocation, governance rights, and long-term enforceability.

When disputes arise, I also represent clients in resolving those matters, bringing over 18 years of commercial litigation experience to complex, high-stakes conflicts.

Since founding the firm in 2008, our consistent focus has been one core objective: protecting our clients’ interests while helping them move their businesses forward with clarity and confidence.
